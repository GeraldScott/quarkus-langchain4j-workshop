Barkly — Our Founder, Chief Comfort Officer, and the Good Boy Who Started It All

Every dog at Barkly's Bark-a-thon owes their cosy life to one scruffy, silver-muzzled legend: Barkly himself. He is the founder of our company, the face on our logo, and the reason we exist. These days he is also a very tired old gentleman who prefers a sunny patch of lawn to a busy schedule.

Barkly is an older dog now, with a grey beard, wise brown eyes, and the unbothered dignity of someone who has seen a great deal and forgiven most of it. But he was not always so comfortable. Years ago he was found as a stray, alone on a pavement in Eersterust, a suburb on the east side of Pretoria, with no collar, no name, and no idea that his luck was about to change.

That change was a woman named Celeste. She spotted the thin, hopeful dog on the side of the road, stopped her bakkie, and simply could not drive away. She took him home, fed him, named him Barkly, and gave him the good life he had been waiting for. The two have been inseparable ever since. Barkly's Bark-a-thon was born from that single act of kindness — the belief that every dog, and every person, deserves comfort, company, and a second chance.

Barkly is a creature of very firm habits. He adores Marie biscuits and biltong, and he will campaign shamelessly for both at every opportunity. When it comes to proper meals, though, he is gloriously fussy: he will only eat chicken necks and mince, and only from The Twins, the local shop Celeste has been sent to more times than she can count. Offer him anything else and he will look at you as though you have personally let him down.

His most treasured possessions are his toys, of which he has an embarrassing number. Barkly is a committed burier — squeaky balls, knotted ropes, and the occasional slipper all disappear into secret holes around the garden, to be rediscovered weeks later (by him, triumphantly, or by Celeste, with a garden fork).

But if you ask Barkly what he loves most in the whole world, the answer is a drive. Nothing delights him more than climbing into Celeste's bakkie, planting himself at the window, and watching Pretoria roll by with the wind in his beard — all while proudly carrying his little purse in his mouth. Nobody is quite sure what he keeps in that purse. Barkly isn't telling.

Please note: Barkly is our founder and mascot, not a bookable support dog. He has earned his retirement and spends his days supervising the team, napping in the sun, and taste-testing biltong. When you book a session, you will be paired with one of our wonderful working Companions — every one of whom Barkly has personally sniffed and approved.

About the company. Barkly's Bark-a-thon Emotional Support Dog Services is a proudly Pretoria-based business, run day to day by our human co-founder, Celeste, with Barkly as our four-legged founder and heart. We serve clients across Gauteng, from Hatfield and Brooklyn to Silver Lakes, Faerie Glen, and beyond. Our mission is simple: to share the calming, healing, unconditional love of a good dog with people who need it. Our office and kennel are open Monday to Saturday, 08:00 to 17:00, and sessions run seven days a week by appointment, booked through our website or mobile app.
