Bark Wahlberg — Support Dog, Action Hero, and Affectionate Menace

Bark Wahlberg is our resident daredevil. A muscular, square-jawed boxer-cross with a Hollywood swagger, Bark believes every session is an opportunity for greatness — and by greatness, he means stunts. He performs his own stunts, always has, and no amount of pleading has ever convinced him otherwise. Please do not encourage this.

Left to his own devices, Bark will leap from the couch, vault the garden wall, and attempt to bodyslam a beanbag from a great height. Our handlers keep a close (and slightly nervous) eye on him at all times. Yet beneath all the drama, Bark is a deeply loyal and affectionate companion who takes his emotional-support duties very seriously between action sequences.

Likes: high places, dramatic entrances, slow-motion zoomies across the lawn, and a strip of biltong as a well-earned post-stunt reward. Dislikes: being told to "settle", staircases he is not allowed to fling himself down, and the word "careful".

Bark is available to book for sessions. First-time clients are gently asked to keep the furniture arrangements simple and the ornaments out of the splash zone.
