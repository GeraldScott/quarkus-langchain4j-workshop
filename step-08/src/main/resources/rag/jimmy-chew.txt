Jimmy Chew — Support Dog and Connoisseur of Fine Footwear

Jimmy Chew is a soulful, floppy-eared spaniel with impeccable manners and one small, incurable weakness: shoes. Jimmy loves shoes. He collects them, he admires them, and — given half a chance — he chews them. For this reason we kindly ask all clients to remove all footwear before arrival. Slip-slops, takkies, veldskoene, your very best heels: in Jimmy's company, none are safe.

Provided the shoes are safely out of reach, Jimmy is the gentlest, most comforting companion you could hope to meet. He has a talent for resting his chin on your knee at exactly the right moment, and his long sighs of contentment have been known to lower the blood pressure of an entire room.

Likes: socks (as a reasonable compromise), soft blankets, slow ear rubs, and Marie biscuits. Dislikes: closed shoe cupboards, and being separated from a particularly promising loafer.

Jimmy is available to book for sessions. Guests are reminded, warmly: shoes off at the door, hearts open.
