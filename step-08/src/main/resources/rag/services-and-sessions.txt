Barkly's Bark-a-thon — Services, Sessions and Pricing

We offer a range of emotional-support-dog sessions to suit every mood, budget, and level of need. Every session is held with one of our licensed Companion dogs and a trained handler, either at our Pretoria premises or, for selected packages, in the comfort of your own home.

Our session types:

The Quick Cuddle (30 minutes) — a short, restorative visit with a support dog. Perfect for a mid-week lift when you just need a warm, waggy friend for a while. R180 per session.

The Classic Bark-a-thon (60 minutes) — our signature session and most popular choice: a full hour of companionship, gentle play, and deep calm. R320 per session.

The Long Walk (90 minutes) — a relaxed outdoor session with a stroll through a nearby Pretoria park, weather and Companion permitting. R450 per session.

The Home Visit (60 minutes) — a Companion and handler come to you, anywhere in the greater Pretoria area. R520 per session, with travel included within 25 km.

The Puppy Package (five 60-minute sessions) — a discounted bundle of five Classic Bark-a-thon sessions. R1 400 in total, saving you R200.

How booking works. You can book through our website or mobile app. All bookings are subject to dog availability, and every first-time client must complete a mandatory sniff test so your chosen Companion can get to know you before your first full session. Please bring your best manners and, in keeping with tradition, a small offering of Marie biscuits and biltong.

Snacks and conduct. Handlers must have snacks on hand at all times — this is not negotiable. Please never tell a Companion they are going to the park and then take them to the vet. Our dogs are for comfort and companionship only, and may never be used for guarding, herding, working-dog duties, or racing.

Cancellations. Reservations can be cancelled up to 11 days before the start of your booking period. If your booking period is shorter than 4 days, it cannot be cancelled. A sudden case of the zoomies, yours or the dog's, is not grounds for cancellation.

Payment. We accept card, EFT, and SnapScan. All prices are in South African Rand and include as many tail wags as you can reasonably handle.
