Mary Puppins — Support Dog, Practically Perfect in Every Way

Mary Puppins arrives precisely when she is needed and, true to her name, departs when the wind changes. A poised, elegant dog with a no-nonsense kind of warmth, Mary has a gift for making everything feel calmer and more orderly the moment she trots into a room. Spoonful-of-sugar energy, minus the sugar — she far prefers biltong.

Mary is wonderful with clients who feel overwhelmed. She has a way of tidying the emotional room: settling the nerves, steadying the breathing, and quietly reminding you that everything is, in fact, going to be all right. Then, when her work is done and the highveld breeze shifts, she gathers herself with great dignity and moves along to the next soul who needs her.

Likes: order, gentle routines, umbrella-shaped shade on a hot afternoon, and a tidy garden. Dislikes: chaos, tardiness, and staying a single moment longer than necessary.

Mary is available to book for sessions. She cannot promise exactly how long she will stay — that, as always, is up to the wind.
