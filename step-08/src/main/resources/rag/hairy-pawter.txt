Hairy Pawter — Support Dog and Certified Good Magic

Hairy Pawter is a bright-eyed young dog with a distinctive marking on his forehead that has launched a thousand questions. Yes, it looks a little like a lightning bolt. No, it is not a scar from a dark wizard. We must stress, for the record: the forehead marking is cosmetic only. Hairy has no magical powers, no secret destiny, and no owl. He is simply a very handsome dog with excellent bone structure.

What Hairy does have is an almost supernatural gift for knowing exactly who in the room needs him most. He will cross an entire garden to sit beside the quietest, most overwhelmed person there. Clients often say a session with Hairy feels a little bit like magic — the ordinary, wonderful kind.

Likes: adventures, making new friends, chasing (but never quite catching) the doves, and Marie biscuits broken into bite-size pieces. Dislikes: baths, and being asked to "do a spell".

Hairy is available to book for sessions. Scarves and house colours are entirely optional.
